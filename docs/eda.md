# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Summary

## Line Plots
- SP500 shows long-term growth with short-term drops.
- Real Price tracks SP500 closely after adjusting for inflation.
- PE10 fluctuates cyclically, indicating valuation cycles.

## Correlations
- SP500 vs Real Price: very high (~0.95)
- SP500 vs PE10: moderate (~0.6)

## Histograms
- SP500 & Real Price: right-skewed
- PE10: peaks in cyclical intervals

## Key Insights
- SP500 and Real Price move together, inflation-adjusted trends mirror the nominal index.
- PE10 shows potential leading indicators for valuation trends.
